## What it is
Alzheimer’s disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects memory, thinking, and behavior.

## What happens in the brain
- Neurons begin to lose connection with each other
- Protein buildup disrupts normal brain function
- Communication between brain cells becomes impaired

## Key features researchers study
- Amyloid beta plaques
- Tau protein tangles
- Loss of neurotransmitter activity

## Why it matters for medicinal chemistry
Medicinal chemistry aims to design molecules that can interact with biological targets in the brain and potentially slow or modify disease progression.
